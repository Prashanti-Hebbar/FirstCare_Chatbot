FirstCare Chatbot

Welcome to the FirstCare Chatbot

This chatbot is designed to provide you with immediate first aid information and assistance. Whether you're dealing with a minor injury or need guidance in an emergency, our chatbot is here to help.

Features of FirstCare Chatbot:

  • Instant First Aid Advice: Get step-by-step instructions on how to handle common injuries such as cuts, burns, and sprains.
  • Emergency Guidance: Receive crucial advice on what to do in case of severe injuries and emergencies.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Provide details about your condition to receive tailored first aid tips and suggestions.
  • Nearby Hospital Locator: Find the nearest hospitals and medical facilities based on your current location.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy-to-use chat interface designed to quickly guide you through first aid procedures.
